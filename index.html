Ubunifu Studios Website

UX/UI Design & Branding

Ubunifu Studios is a creative agency website that embodies innovation and artistic excellence. The design showcases a modern, clean aesthetic with bold typography and strategic use of whitespace to highlight the studio's creative portfolio and services. View on Figma.

Coffee Shop website

UX Design

For the Coffee Shop website, I created a cozy, inviting design with rich colors and engaging visuals. Using Figma and Adobe XD, I developed responsive wireframes and prototypes, resulting in a user-friendly site that highlights the charm and offerings of the coffee shop across all devices.

Funiture mobile app

UX Design

For the Furniture mobile app, I crafted a sleek, user-friendly interface that highlights the elegance and variety of furniture pieces. The design is fully responsive, ensuring a smooth experience on all mobile devices. Using Figma and Adobe XD, I developed detailed wireframes and prototypes, resulting in an intuitive app that makes browsing and purchasing furniture effortless and enjoyable.

Finvo pos System website

UX Design

Designed for clarity and efficiency, this website presents the Finvo POS System with a user-friendly interface and engaging visuals. It highlights key features, benefits, and functionalities, making it easy for users to understand and explore the system’s capabilities.
